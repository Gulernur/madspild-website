The fight against food waste!

Food waste is a global issue and in Denmark the service sector alone contributes to over 227.000 ton of waste each year. In Denmark alone, 2.000 ton of food is thrown out every single day.

World hunger is a big issue yet 1/3 of food production globally, is going to waste. We are partnering with multiple international organisations and civil society to raise awareness of this global issue.
